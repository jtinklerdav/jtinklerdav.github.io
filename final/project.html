Wine Trends in the 21st Century

Map of global consumption in 2020

This graph is highlighting the difference in wine consumption across the globe, it can be seen that the more economically developed countries tend to have a greater consumption.

Map of global exports in 2021

This map highlights the key wine exporting countries, it can be seen that countries in the milder regions (South and North) produce a greater volume of wine, this may be due to climate conditions.

To analyse the trends in the 21st Century I am going to focus on 9 countries: Algeria, Argentina, Australia, China, France, Italy, South Africa, United Kingdom and The United States of America. These countries have been chosen as they represent most global regions, 'Old World Wine producers' & 'New World wine Producers' as well as key importers and developing countries.
